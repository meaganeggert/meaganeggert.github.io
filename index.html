The Pagcaliuagans

It all started in 1961 when Leonard & Priscilla Pagcaliuagan made it official. Since then they had three children -- Leonard, Mario, and Sabrina. They all started families of their own and now they have a total of 21 direct descendants. Meagan (Leonard & Priscilla's granddaughter) started this page to share recipes, since the most obvious way that Leonard and Priscilla show love is through food.
While recipe sharing is great, we also wanted to be able to keep in touch with the entire family and see what everyone has been up to, so we added individual pages to learn about each family and also photo galleries to see everyone's beautiful faces.
